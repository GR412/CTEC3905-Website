GAMEMODES

Survival

About The Gamemode

Survival mode is the gamemode of Minecraft, in which players must collect resources, build structures, battle mobs, manage hunger, and explore the land in an effort to survive

In Survival, players have an inventory in which they may gather items. These items may be combined using certain recipes to create tools and other items. This process is known as crafting. Most crafting recipes need a crafting table. Players are also able to create potions by brewing

Difficulty And Commands

On CRAFT412, Survival is set to normal difficulty, with added plugins to enable a user to issue commands for a more enjoyable experience

All available commands for this gamemode are listed with the command /help

Spawn/Safe Area

There is also a spawn/safe area, in which PVP is disabled and mobs are restricted. Within the spawn/safe area there are rooms that provide the following:

  • Portal Room - Can take you to the Nether and the End
  • Bank Vaults - A vault can be purchased with in game currency to store valuable items
  • Crafting Room - Allows crafting in the form of Smelting, Crafting, Repairing/Renaming and Enchantment
  • Jail - For them wretched rule breakers!

Building Restrictions

Building rights are restricted 50 blocks after the spawn/safe area perimeter wall (This is to stop players building too close to the spawn/safe area)
